A Portfolio Storytelling Activity!

One of the biggest mistakes we see in portfolios is focusing on the pixels rather than the overall story. This exercise will give you a set of tools to help you to critique your peers' portfolios as well as tell your own story.

Activity Intro

In groups of three, you'll take on different roles and partake in a group discussion. You'll do three rounds of the activity so each person can play the different roles. This site will help facilitate you through your activity.

For each round, decide who in your group will be the Designer, Storyteller, and Hiring Manager. Below are the descriptions of the roles. You can click on each for instructions on how to play that role.

  • Clickable nav links that smooth scroll to page sections
  • Responsive behavior when clicking nav links perfect for a one page website
  • Bootstrap's scrollspy feature which highlights which section of the page you're on in the navbar
  • Minimal custom CSS so you are free to explore your own unique design options
